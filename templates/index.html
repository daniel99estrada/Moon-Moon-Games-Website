About Us

In 2023, Salvadoran developer Daniel Estrada founded Moon Moon Games, an indie game studio focused on creating stylized mobile games and VR experiences with exceptional music and sound effects. Daniel's passion for game design, coupled with his strong programming and musical background, drives us to create games that are engaging and stimulating. We are excited to share our passion for games with you.
